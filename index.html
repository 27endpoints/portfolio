About Me

As a Django AI Prototype Architect (DAIPA), I specialize in transforming product concepts into functional prototypes with unprecedented speed and precision.

With expertise in Django framework and advanced AI prompt engineering, I bridge the gap between product vision and technical feasibility. My approach focuses on creating functional Proof-of-Concept applications that validate ideas quickly, enabling faster decision-making and reducing development risks.

I leverage cutting-edge AI tools alongside Django's powerful capabilities to deliver working prototypes in days rather than weeks, helping businesses test assumptions, gather user feedback, and make data-driven decisions about product development.
